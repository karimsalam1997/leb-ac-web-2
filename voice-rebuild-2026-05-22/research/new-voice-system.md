# Lebanese Academic Voice System, Version 1

This is the working system for the rewritten essay folder.

## The Core Shift

Old problem: the essays often argue correctly, but too cleanly. They explain the Lebanese machine from above, as if the writer is standing over a diagram.

New rule: the writer starts inside the machine. A street, a bill, a shrine, a souk, a bank account, a broken elevator, a generator line, an old map. The structure is still there, but the reader meets it through pressure.

## Sentence Rhythm

Every paragraph should feel slightly alive.

- One sentence can run long if it carries memory, place, and consequence.
- One sentence should be blunt enough to leave a bruise.
- Avoid the school-essay staircase: claim, explanation, example, implication, conclusion.
- Avoid the assistant staircase: "This is why," "What emerges," "At its core," "This matters because."
- Use repetition only when it sounds like insistence, not padding.

## Paragraph Shape

The preferred paragraph shapes are:

Scene to verdict.

Concession to cut.

Object to system.

Memory to accusation.

Historical fact to present cost.

The forbidden paragraph shape is:

Abstract claim, balanced caveat, broader implication, polished landing.

## Diction

Use normal words.

Say "is" when something is something.

Say "sold," "buried," "priced," "blocked," "protected," "emptied," "counted," "refused," "fed," "paid," "waited," "called," "signed," "forgot," "remembered."

Do not use grand nouns unless a real object is beside them.

## Lebanese Proof Rule

Every paragraph needs at least one anchor:

- a person
- a street
- a building
- a date
- an institution
- a law
- a village
- a ritual
- an object
- a phrase people actually say

If the paragraph has none, it probably belongs to an assistant.

## Argument Rule

Concede the strongest opposing view in plain language.

Then answer it without politeness theater.

Example:

Rafic Hariri did build. That is what makes the argument harder. A ruined center was not going to repair itself by prayer. But building is not innocence, and cranes do not absolve the legal machinery that turned a city center into a financial instrument.

## Ending Rule

Do not close with instructions to society.

Close with a thing.

A balcony light.

A census table.

A blocked street.

A shrine.

A child learning which office needs a phone call before the form can move.
